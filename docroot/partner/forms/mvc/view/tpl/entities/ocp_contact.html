Privacy Policy – Partner Application

Organizational part of the Agency entrusted with the processing of personal data

Andrew Smith, Head of Communication and Promotion Unit

Purpose of processing

The data requested on this form is collected for the purpose of selecting the most appropriate candidates that will become Official Campaign Partners and for displaying the professional data of those organisations on the Healthy Workplaces Campaign website, as well as to create a user profile to access the “Private Area of the Official Campaign Partners”. Your personal data will also be stored in EU-OSHA’s contact’s database which EU-OSHA uses for the purpose of managing partnerships and working relations with stakeholders at the Agency, sending invitations to Agency events and dissemination of Agency publications and other relevant information. It might also be used for surveys to obtain feedback on the Agency’s performance in relation to the Annual Management Plan and the Multi-annual Strategic Programme. It will also be transferred to contractors who will manage different communications related to campaign activities on behalf of EU-OSHA in your country, like newsletters, news, invitations to events or surveys.

Type of data processed

The personal data displayed on the website includes:
• Name and picture of the CEO
• Name and address of the enterprise
• Name and email of the contact person
• Name of the health and safety representative
• Telephone and fax numbers
This profile will enable you to edit all the data entered about your enterprise, as well as to access specific data about the campaign activities.
The data requested on the form includes:
• Company / Organisation name
• Address
• General email address
• General telephone number
• General fax number
• Name/Surname and position of the main contact person for the Campaign
• Email address and telephone number of main contact person
• Name, email address and telephone number of the health and safety representative
• Name and picture of the CEO
By submitting this form you agree that EU-OSHA transfers your data to third party contractors/partners, and displays your data on the Healthy Workplaces Campaign website, and commit yourself to keep this information correct and up to date for the duration of the campaign.

Legal basis

Council Regulation (EC) No. 2062/94 of 18/07/1994 establishing the European Agency for Safety and Health at Work and subsequent amendments.

Lawfulness of processing

The processing is based on Article 5(a) of Regulation (EC) No. 45/2001 of the European Parliament and the Council of 18 December 2000 on the protection of individuals with regard to the processing of personal data by the Community institutions and bodies and on the free movement of such data (hereinafter, Regulation (EC) No. 45/2001).

Data recipients

Access to the personal data is granted on the basis of the role and responsibilities of the subjects involved (“need to know” principle):
• Duly appointed EU-OSHA staff
• External provider hosting and maintaining EU-OSHA’s server
• Legal Service, Civil Service Tribunal, the European Data Protection Supervisor, the European Anti-Fraud Office (OLAF), the European Ombudsman, the Court of Auditors, the Internal Audit Service, if applicable.
All the recipients mentioned are bound by Regulation (EC) Nº 45/2001 of the European Parliament and of the Council on the protection of individuals with regard to the processing of personal data by the Community institutions and bodies and on the free movement of such data. EU-OSHA will not divulge personal data for direct marketing for commercial purposes.

The data subject’s rights

Healthy Workplaces Campaign partners can access their personal information, verify its accuracy, delete it and correct it by logging into the system. However, if you have any questions about the Healthy Workplaces Campaign, which are not addressed here or in the public section of the official campaign website, please contact us at: information@osha.europa.eu, including in the subject the words “data protection”.

Information on the conservation period of data

This data related to the partners is kept until the finalisation of the campaign.

Security measures

We take appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorised access to or unauthorised alteration, disclosure or destruction of data. These include internal reviews of our data collection, storage and processing practices and security measures, including appropriate encryption of communication and physical security measures to guard against unauthorised access to systems where we store personal data.

Request for information

For any further information regarding the handling of their personal data, data subjects can address their request to EU-OSHA Data Protection Officer at: dpo@osha.europa.eu.

Recourse to the EDPS

Should you have queries or complaints regarding the collecting, processing or use of your personal data, please contact the Data Protection Officer at DPO@osha.europa.eu. In case of conflict, complaints can also be addressed to the European Data Protection Supervisor.
